1. Introduction

Spry ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Life Coach AI mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (collected via Firebase Authentication)

Profile Data

  • Name, age, and gender
  • Focus areas and interests
  • Personality assessment results

Usage Data

  • Mood check-ins
  • Chat messages with the AI coach
  • Task completions
  • Journal entries
  • Assessment responses (PHQ-9, detailed intake)

Device Information

  • Language preference
  • Device type (collected solely for app functionality)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Personalize your AI coaching sessions
  • Generate CBT-based tasks tailored to your needs
  • Track your mood patterns and progress over time
  • Improve overall app functionality and user experience

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Your personal data — including profile information, mood check-ins, chat history, tasks, and assessment results — is stored locally on your device using SQLite.
  • Only your email address is stored on Firebase servers for the purpose of authentication.
  • No personal health data is uploaded to cloud servers.
  • Chat session context is sent to the Google Gemini API to generate AI responses, but this data is not stored by us on any server.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

Firebase Authentication (Google)

Used for email-based login only. Your email address is processed and stored by Firebase to manage your account.

Google Gemini API

Processes your chat messages to generate AI coaching responses. Messages are sent on a per-session basis and are not retained by us after the session ends.

RevenueCat

Manages in-app subscriptions and purchase processing. RevenueCat processes purchase data in accordance with their own privacy policy.

Ad Networks

Free-tier users may see advertisements within the App. Ad partners may collect device identifiers in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. The only data shared with third-party services is limited to what is described in the "Third-Party Services" section above, and only to the extent necessary to provide the App's functionality.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access your data — Use the export feature within the App to download your information.
  • Delete your data — Use the delete feature within the App to remove all locally stored data from your device.
  • Delete your account — Deleting your account removes your Firebase authentication record and associated email.
  • Opt out of ads — Upgrade to a premium subscription to remove advertisements.

8. Children's Privacy

Life Coach AI is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 18. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.
